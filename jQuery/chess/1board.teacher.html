Chess board

  • Setup the HTML, CSS, and images

Goal

    • As a user, select a chess piece to highlight the square with a red outline.
    • As a dev, loop through all the chess pieces and bind a click event. Handle the click by applying a CSS rule of border: red solid 2px
  2. As a user, selecting various chess pieces will only hightlight one square at a time
  3. As a dev, pressed arrow keys should be detected and logged in the console
  4. As a user, pressing the arrow keys will move the highlighted chess piece without ristriction of board. Move highlighted piece one square per key (up, down, right, left)
  5. As a user, pressing the arrow keys will move the highlighted chess piece within the boundary of the chess board.
  6. As a user, pressing the arrow keys will move the highlighted chess piece with animation within the boundary of the chess board.
